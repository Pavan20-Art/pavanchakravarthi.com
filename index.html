About

A designer who cares about the details.

I work at the intersection of brand and product — building consistent visual systems, polished UI, and marketing collateral that hit tight deadlines without compromising quality.

My approach is methodical: I study the brief, audit the brand, sketch broadly, then narrow to the simplest expression that tells the story. I’m comfortable owning a project end‑to‑end and collaborating closely with developers, marketing and stakeholders.
